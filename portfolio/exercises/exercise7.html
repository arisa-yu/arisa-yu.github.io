Graphic/UX Design Portfolio

Microsoft Word Manual

I created a Microsoft Word Manual, which consists of 22 pages, that focused on educating college students on the tools needed to build their resume. The final product was an accumulation of user research I performed, many module drafts, and technical writing knowledge I acquired throughout the ENG 380 course.

View my Word Manual! (JPG)

Magazine Layout

For my Art 264 (digital design) course, I took text from Milton Glaser's Ten Things I Have Learned to create a magazine layout, utilizing Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva. This project, which consists of 13 pages, combined the different design concepts I learned throughout the course like color theory, typography, layout, and more.
